Carnage Rulebook

Rule 1: Transfer Lock Deadlines

Transfer guidelines follow official Premier League timestamps. Mid-gameweek transfer violations or community trade-override rules configured by administrators must be respected. Failure to pay entrance fees before GW1 locks results in automatic strikes.

Rule 2: Disciplinary Strike Policy

Members receive active strikes for: continuous late check-ins, abusive conduct within community chats, failure to fulfill challenge stakes, or score tampering. Accumulated strikes result in dynamic lockouts.

Rule 3: Stake Validation & Payout

Weekly matchup challenges and monthly standings stakes must be transferred within 24 hours of confirmation. Standard transactions must include administrative approvals with verified logs.
